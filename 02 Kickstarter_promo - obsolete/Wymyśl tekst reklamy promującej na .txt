Introducing the DualTech CNC, the revolutionary CNC machine controller that will take your projects to the next level. With the ability to work seamlessly with Mach3, DrufelCNC, and open-source programs like GRBL, including Universal Gcode Sender, Lightburn, and Candle, this is a truly unique solution. The DualTech CNC allows for the simultaneous operation of these two protocols, something that has not been possible until now.

One of the key advantages of the DualTech CNC is its ability to control multiple tools. This includes a spindle for cutting in 4 axes, as well as the option to connect a laser. Whether you're a hobbyist or a semi-professional, the possibilities are endless with this machine.

Built on ARM microcontrollers, the DualTech CNC is versatile and easy to use. It can be used for milling, cutting wood, prototyping PCBs, precise drilling, and much more. With laser capabilities, it's perfect for engraving, cutting, etching photos, decorating surfaces, and creating boxes.

The DualTech CNC is compatible with Windows, Linux, and Mac OS, as well as PC and Raspberry Pi. The only requirement is that your preferred software can be installed on the system and platform.

Not only is this machine incredibly versatile, but it's also cost-effective. By using a single controller to operate Mach3 and GRBL, you can significantly reduce your workshop equipment costs. And the satisfaction of having such a versatile tool in your arsenal is priceless.

Our prototype has undergone rigorous testing, and based on our analysis and FMEA, we have improved the project's documentation. The next step is to finalize the documentation and distribute it to our customers.
